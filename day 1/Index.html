How to apreciate an art exposition

  1. First of all, you have to be polite and don't scream.
  2. You shouldn't touch the art, unless you are invited to do this, there are some expositions that do this.
  3. Don't try to "understand" everything. Sometimes a cigar is just a cigar...
  4. Fell free to take your time. You don't need to run or follow other peoples rythm.
  5. If you follow those adivices you probably will not have problems in galerys and will have a great experience!
